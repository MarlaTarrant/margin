What we do

We are an economic think-tank & consultancy specializing in ecologically sustainable development.

Margineco developed the EcoRatingTM system to amalgamate the world of business and environmentalism, ensuring corporations meet and adhere to their sustainable development goals; and in so doing, we created the EcoNetTM, a network of like-minded clients, entrepreneurs, and investors all working together to redefine business conduct.

By Joining the EcoNetTM you will receive full access to Margineco’s economists and specialists, as well as some of the best business opportunities in your industry, including clients, businesses, and investors who put environmentalism and sustainable business first.
Get your EcoRatingTM Today!

Angels

Investor access to the EcoNetTM is exclusively available to government, angel investors and philanthropists, who gain access to relevant in-depth information on our business partners who wish to open themselves to opportunities including investments, government tenders and donations according to their level of EcoRatingTM compliance.

Business

Join an exclusive community and gain full access to the EcoNetTM granting you special access to tenders, investors and clients who share your businesses vision. Your EcoRatingTM will be ranked and displayed accordingly. You will also have full access to Margineco’s specialists who have your best interests at heart.

Clientele

Visitors access to the EcoNetTM allowing you to connect with like-minded businesses, ensuring you deal only with companies who are guaranteed to have a positive environmental impact.

Our Process

Helping you grow.

Audit

The first step towards obtaining your EcoRatingTM is our extensive on-site audit. We fully analyze your business and its operations according to our unique framework. Our audit implements a specially weighted system, designed specifically for your business.

Rate

Our panel of experts will analyze and review all data obtained during your audit. We then provide you with a preliminary and confidential rating, that corresponds with your business’s current levels of compliance. You are then given the option to publish your rating and join the EcoNetTM, or allow us to advise you on ways you can improve your rating.

Advise

If you would like to improve your EcoRatingTM, we will advise you on how you can reduce your company’s environmental impact, and begin changing the way you conduct business. We will also give you full access to our panel of experts and an in-depth report that will function as a guideline for your company to follow. Additionally, we will recommend a dynamic time-frame within which to make these amendments.
