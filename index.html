Market Intelligence

Our market reports are developed for investors and businesses looking to secure a competitive edge in the various economic sectors internationally. Each report provides an overview of the market, including an overview of general or specific investment opportunities where applicable.

Industry Forecasts

Macroeconomic analysis of an applicable economic sector at an international or national level, as well as reports on how the economy may impact the different industry segments. Predictability is relevant to all organizations as it builds confidence and certainty in an economy.

Financial Management

Our analysis measures, in monetary terms, the benefits of a specific project on the economy and target market. Additionally, it measures an economic system, production process or industry to determine if it is operating effectively and how much profit it is making.

What is Unity?

Unity is a dynamic, applied economic theory and initiative, currently in development by Margineco. Unity is also the topic of Taylor’s PhD, and our life’s purpose.

What do Economists do?

We study how efficiently a society makes use of its resources, and develop policies and theory to improve this. We also conduct research, collect data, monitor economic trends and develop market forecasts across all sectors and industries that make up the economy.

How we can help you

In this trying time, we know and understand the fear and uncertainty of not knowing what lies ahead. Thankfully this is our specialty, at Margineco we are geared to assist you at any level, whether you are an individual looking to work out an efficient home budget, a business owner looking to manage risk, sustainability and profit, or an organization in need of a market analysis or industry forecast to keep up in this dynamic economy, at Margineco we’ve got your back.
