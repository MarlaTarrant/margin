What we do

We are an economic think-tank & consultancy specializing in ecologically sustainable development for business.

EcoRatingTM

We developed the EcoRatingTM system to actionably amalgamate the world of business and ecological awareness, ensuring businesses meet and adhere to sustainable development goals to profit.

EcoWiseTM

We built EcoWiseTM as a platform for business to take advantage of our knowledge and give them the competitive edge they need to thrive, while giving back to the environment.

EcoNetTM

In so doing, we created the EcoNetTM, to meet a demand for a network of like-minded individuals, businesses, and organizations all working together to redefine business conduct.

Our Process

Helping you grow.

Audit

The first step towards obtaining your EcoRatingTM is our extensive on-site audit. We fully analyze your business and its operations according to our unique framework. Our audit implements an industry-based weighted system, designed especially for your business.

Assess

Our panel of experts review all data obtained during your audit. We then provide you with a preliminary and confidential rating, that corresponds with your business’s current levels of compliance. You are then given the opportunity to publish your rating and join the EcoNetTM, or allow us to advise you on ways you can improve your rating.

Advise

If you would like to improve your EcoRatingTM, we will advise you on how you can reduce your company’s environmental impact, and begin changing the way you conduct business. We will also give you full access to our panel of experts and an in-depth report that will function as a guideline for your company to follow. Additionally, we will recommend a dynamic time-frame within which to make these amendments.

What is the Luxembourg100?

The Luxembourg100 is a local initiative to launch Margineco in Luxembourg and raise awareness to the principles that define us, and what we have to offer.

How it works

We offer full, free EcoRatings to the first 100 businesses in Luxembourg, who will then join the EcoNetTM as honorary members.

The Luxembourg100 will include an on-site audit and full assessment, which includes your EcoRatingTM, free of charge. Additionally, the Luxembourg100 will get a discount when using our consultants.

Contact us for more information.

Why are we doing this?

We have a passion for the environment and we want the Earth to be around for future generations. You cannot eat money, and while it may “make the world go round”, too often it is used to the detriment of the environment. We aim to change that.

Does it cost anything?

If you are part of the Luxembourg100, absolutely nothing, gratis, no strings attached.

We do ask, however, in support of Launching Margineco in Luxembourg, that you consider spreading the word and help us make a difference.
